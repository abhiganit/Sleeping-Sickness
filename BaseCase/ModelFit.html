1 Model Parameters

We will estimate three parameters: the transmission probability from humans to tsetse \(\beta_{VH}\), the transmission probability from tsetse to humans \(\beta_H\) and rate of seeking treatment for stage II patients \(\zeta_2\). The rest of parameters will either be fixed or be sampled from their distribution (\(\eta\),\(gamma_{H_i}\), and \(P_{i+/D}\).)
